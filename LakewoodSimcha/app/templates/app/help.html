Where's the Simcha?

The home page features all the events in Lakwood in one convenient location. Full featured calendar can toggle weekends and display day, week or month view for a full picture of all that's going on. To clear some of the clutter, Click the checkboxes next to the event type to hide them.

Clicking on an event will pop up the event details.

Book

Select your venue type to see the availability on the Book page. For example, booking a Wedding hall will privide access to all the current bookings in the available wedding halls. Many of the dates are tentative and will display "Tentative" with a red border.

Bookings will not display their information on the Book page until confirmed by the venue. Similarly, they will not appear on the Where's the Simcha? page until confirmed.

Click on any date to create a tentative booking. You must provide an email address and phone number. The email and phone will be used to identify the booking as yours. When a booking is created it will send an email both to you and the venue, with contact information. You must contact the venue to finalize the booking.

Your tentative booking can be moved to another date by dragging/dropping. You will need to provide your email/phone to verify the booking is yours.

Tentative bookings will expire at the end of the booking duration set by the venue. A warning email will be sent prior to expirtaion. Expired bookings will be removed and the process will need to be repeateed.

Clicking on an event will pop up the event details.
