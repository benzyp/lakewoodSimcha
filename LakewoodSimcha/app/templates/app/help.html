Where's the Simcha?

The home page features all the events in Lakwood in one convenient location. Full featured calendar can toggle weekends, display day, week or month for a full picture of all that's going on. To clear some of the clutter, Click the checkboxes next to the event type to hide thm.

Clicking on an event will pop up the event details.

Book

Select your venue type to see the availability on the Book page. For example, booking a Wedding hall will privide access to all the current bookings in the available wedding halls. Many of the dates are tentative symbolized by a red border and no title.

Bookings will not display their information on the Book page until confirmed. Similarly, they will not appear on the Where's the Simcha? page until confirmed.

Clicking on an event will pop up the event details.
